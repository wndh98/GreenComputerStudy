Welcome to the Joint Symposium on Quantum Computing

August 24-25, 2023, 9:00AM - 6:00PM

Baekyangro Underground, Lah Jeh Kun Hall(B147), Yonsei University, Seoul , Korea

The Joint Symposium on Quantum Computing 2023 is an inaugural conference co-organized by the Yonsei University Institute of Quantum
Information Technology, the Keio University Quantum Computing Center, and the National Taiwan University Center for Quantum Science and
Engineering and National Center for Theoretical Sciences.
The main theme of JSQC2023 will be the scientific research activities focused onthe applications and use cases of quantum computing.
We want to share new developments in this rapidly changing field and to establish new friendships, interests, and collaborations.
We hope this inaugural conference develops to an annual series of prestigious symposium in the future.
